The Css box model is 4 boxes within each other with all of them having different functions. Content is the box that holds the text and images. Padding is the space between the content and the border which is transparent, the border is self explanatory. Margin is the outside spade of the border which is also transparent.

The most important part of css is positioning. There are 6 different types that you can choose from static, relative, fixed, absolute, initial, and inherit. Static goes with the flow of the page and is not affected by any attributes. Relative position would go where it normaly would and you can add attributes to it such as left/right etc. Fixed position will stay on the same spot on the page no matter what. Absolute position will be placed closests to the previous positioning.

The article tag is for repeatable/reuseable tags for blogs, forums, posts etc or a movie list. Also a ".div name attribute" will target and only affect those attribute inside the div. The navigation bar can be made as a list(ul-unordered list li-list item) or as a table(tr-table row th-table headings).
