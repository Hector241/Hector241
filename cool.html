The Css box model is 4 boxes within each other with all of them having different functions. Content is the box that holds the text and images. Padding is the space between the content and the border which is transparent, the border is self explanatory. Margin is the outside spade of the border which is also transparent.

The most important part of css is positioning. There are 6 different
types that you can choose from static, relative, fixed, absolute,
initial, and inherit. Static goes with the flow of the page and is
not affected by any attributes. Relative position would go where it
normaly would and you can add attributes to it such as left/right etc.
Fixed position will stay on the same spot on the page no matter what.
absolute position can be place where ever you want it on the page.

The article tag is a special tag that lets you make h2-h6 reuseable when you need it in a sequence Eg. the movie list on the other tab.
Also a div.img img or .img img will target img elements/tags inside the div.
